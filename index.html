> PALANTIR_TECHNOLOGIES // WORK_LOG  _

> What I appreciate about Palantir's brand voice is the unapologetic nature of it. They say what they mean. And they mean it.

> Basically my job is to push our voice as far from Accenture, AWS, and McKinsey as possible. I enjoy dunking on stupid corporations.

> ASSET_TYPE: OUT-OF-HOME / CAMPAIGN

// Billboard Campaign

> wrote copy and concepts for palantir brand billboards. directive: find the PLTR point of view. no tech jargon. make something that respects the reader.

The idea in play here was to revitalize American industrial cities with AI. Palantir makes enterprise software for factories. The design was simple here — what this project really taught me was how to coordinate with OOH ad companies from start to finish.
> ASSET_TYPE: PRINT / EVENT

// Poster Design — Army v Navy Game

> I led design and copy for this one. It was in a pamphlet given to guests at the Army v Navy game.

This was a project I took upon on my own accord to prove my worth as an intern. I wanted to come to the table with a full copy + design product finished.
> ASSET_TYPE: RECRUITMENT / EDITORIAL

// Palantir Recruitment Booklet

> OPEN_DOCUMENT: Palantir_Commercial_Careers_Booklet.pdf

> Recruiting wanted a booklet to hand to potential hires — I led the copy and directed design on this one.

The recruitment team came to me for a hiring project. The goal was to help college age hires understand what working at palantir looks like. I broke down the project into key pages — and designed a story outline to contextualize the different roles Palantir offers. Finally, I worked with the design team to create a tri-fold brochure that can be handed out at college campus events.
